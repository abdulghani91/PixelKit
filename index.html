Welcome to PixelKit. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

PixelKit collects minimal data to provide and improve our services:

2.2 Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your photos or images: All image processing is done locally on your device. We never access, upload, or store your images.
  • Personal identifiers: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • Location data: We do not track your GPS location.
  • Contacts or messages: We do not access your contact list, SMS, or call history.
  • Browsing history: We do not track your web browsing activity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited data we collect for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core image editing, conversion, compression, and other tools.
  • Advertising: To display banner and interstitial ads through Google AdMob. Ads help keep PixelKit free.
  • Analytics: To understand feature usage and prioritize improvements.
  • Crash Reporting: To detect, diagnose, and fix technical issues.
  • App Improvement: To optimize performance and user experience based on aggregated data.

4. Advertising & Google AdMob

PixelKit uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is a mobile advertising platform operated by Google LLC.

4.1 Ad Formats Used

  • Banner Ads: Small ads shown at the bottom of tool screens.
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown occasionally after completing a tool action.
Note: PixelKit does not use rewarded video ads. There are no "watch ad to unlock" or "watch ad for premium features" mechanisms in the app.

4.2 Data Collected by AdMob

Google AdMob may collect and process the following data:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID, Android ID)
  • IP address (for fraud prevention and ad delivery)
  • Device type, OS version, and screen size
  • General location (country/region level, not precise GPS)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions, clicks)

For more information, please review Google's privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policies

6. Permissions We Request

PixelKit requests the following Android permissions:

  • Internet: Required for loading advertisements.
  • Camera: Required to capture photos directly within the app (e.g., Background Remover, Circle Crop, Collage Maker).
  • Read External Storage / Read Media Images: Required to select images from your gallery for processing.
  • Write External Storage: Required to save processed images (edited photos, PDFs, compressed files) to your device storage.
Important: We request the minimum permissions necessary. You can deny any permission, though doing so may limit certain features (e.g., denying Camera prevents in-app photo capture).

7. Data Storage & Security

Local Processing: All image editing, compression, conversion, and other processing occurs entirely on your device. Your images never leave your phone.

Temporary Files: PixelKit may create temporary files during processing (e.g., when exporting a PDF or creating a collage). These are stored in your device's temporary directory and are automatically cleared by the operating system.

Analytics Data: Usage analytics and crash logs are transmitted securely (encrypted in transit via HTTPS) to Google's Firebase servers.

Data Retention: We do not retain personal data. Analytics data is handled according to Google's data retention policies. You can request deletion of your analytics data by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

PixelKit is not designed for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information promptly.
